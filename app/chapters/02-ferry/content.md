We're getting there the slow way — and that's the point.

The [Alaska Marine Highway](https://dot.alaska.gov/amhs/index.shtml) departs Bellingham every **Friday** from May through September, threading 800 miles of fjords, islands, and rainforest up the Inside Passage to Juneau. The journey takes about **62 hours** aboard the MV Columbia, with stops in Ketchikan, Wrangell, and Petersburg along the way.

**Summer 2026 sailings: May 1 – Sept 30.** The [online timetable](https://dot.alaska.gov/amhs/schedules.shtml) has the full calendar; [book directly at AMHS](https://bookamhs.alaska.gov/book/sailing-search/journeySearch) or call (800) 642-0066. Dynamic pricing — book early.

There's a solarium on the upper deck with heaters overhead — bring a sleeping bag and watch the coastline slide by. [Cabins](https://dot.alaska.gov/amhs/fares.shtml) are available too, sold by the room not the person.

Fair warning: crossing **Queen Charlotte Sound** is open ocean. Pack seasickness meds just in case.
