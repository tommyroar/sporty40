We're staying at [Best Western Grandma's Feather Bed](https://www.bestwestern.com/en_US/book/hotels-in-juneau/best-western-grandma-s-feather-bed/propertyCode.02012.html) — a Victorian farmhouse inn tucked into Mendenhall Valley with only 14 rooms.

Suites come with lofty feather beds, in-room whirlpool tubs, and a complimentary full hot breakfast every morning. It's cozy, it's a little ridiculous, and it's perfect.

The location is ideal: Mendenhall Glacier is right up the road, and the hotel runs a free 24-hour shuttle to the airport and ferry terminal.
